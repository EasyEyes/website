EasyEyes

The open-source EasyEyes web app, for use by anyone, will calibrate any display and the viewing distance for online vision testing, including the fundamental visual measurements of acuity, crowding, and contrast sensitivity of diverse populations, small and large. Currently, we have a working prototype with limited features and no documentation.
